Haaze Privacy Policy

Contents

  • Personal info
    • I plan to be completely transparent about everything related to your personal info. To get this straight and clear, the ONLY thing I can see is your email address and the name you provide. I am using FIRAuth authentication so not even I can see your password.
  • Collection
    • Technologies
  • I am currently using Apple's CoreLocation library to get the location of your device for this app. (after your permission of course). The app can only get your location while you are USING the app. The location is then stored in a Parse Server backend (this is done because we need the geo coordinates to calculate distance between users). Your data will not be "sold" or "given" to anybody.
  • Disclosure
    • None of your information is disclosed to anyone.
  • Storage
    • Where your info lives: Firebase Database, Parse Server
